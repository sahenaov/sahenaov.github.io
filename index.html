AR Aircraft

Development of an augmented reality (AR) application designed to enable users to interactively rotate and move a virtual airplane model. This application provides a dynamic and engaging way to explore and understand the basic information related to the airplane's operation and creation. Users can manipulate the airplane in a three-dimensional space, gaining insights into its design and functionality through an immersive AR experience.
